Homemade Pepperoni Pizza

Description

Homemade Pepperoni Pizza is a classic favorite that combines a chewy crust, savory tomato sauce, gooey mozzarella cheese, and spicy pepperoni slices. Making pizza at home allows you to customize toppings, control the ingredients, and enjoy a fresh, hot pizza straight from your oven.

This recipe uses a simple dough, homemade or store-bought tomato sauce, and a generous layer of cheese and pepperoni, making it a delicious and satisfying meal for pizza night.

Ingredients

  • For the Dough:
    • 2 ¼ teaspoons active dry yeast (1 packet)
    • 1 ½ cups warm water (110°F or 45°C)
    • 3 ½ to 4 cups all-purpose flour
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 teaspoon sugar
    • 1 teaspoon salt
  • For the Sauce:
    • 1 cup tomato sauce (homemade or store-bought)
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • 1 teaspoon dried basil
    • 1 clove garlic (minced, optional)
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • For the Topping:
    • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    • 20-30 slices pepperoni
    • Fresh basil leaves (optional for garnish)

Steps

  1. Step 1: Prepare the Dough
    • In a large bowl, dissolve the yeast and sugar in warm water. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until foamy.
    • Add the flour, olive oil, and salt, and mix until a dough forms. Knead for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
    • Place the dough in an oiled bowl, cover, and let it rise for 1-1.5 hours, or until doubled in size.
  2. Step 2: Preheat Oven and Prepare Sauce
    • Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up.
    • In a small bowl, mix the tomato sauce with oregano, basil, minced garlic (if using), salt, and pepper.
  3. Step 3: Shape the Dough
    • Once the dough has risen, punch it down and divide it into 2 portions if you want two pizzas.
    • Roll out the dough on a floured surface into a 12-inch circle or your desired thickness.
  4. Step 4: Assemble the Pizza
    • Place the rolled-out dough on a pizza pan or a lightly floured pizza peel if using a pizza stone.
    • Spread a thin layer of sauce over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
    • Sprinkle mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce and then add pepperoni slices on top.
  5. Step 5: Bake
    • Transfer the pizza to the oven and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  6. Step 6: Serve
    • Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly.
    • Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired, slice, and enjoy your homemade pepperoni pizza!
